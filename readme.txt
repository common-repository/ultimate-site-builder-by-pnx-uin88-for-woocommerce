== Installation ==

= Requirements =

To install Ultimate Site Builder by PNX Uin88 for Woocommerce plugin for your woocommerce store, you need:

* WordPress Version 5.3 or newer (installed)
* PHP Version 7.1 or newer

= Setup and Configuration =

Follow the steps below to connect the plugin to your UIN88 account:

1. After you have activated the UUltimate Site Builder by PNX Uin88 for Woocommerce Plugin, go to the menu of "**Ultimate Site Builder for Woocommerce by PNX Uin88**.
2. Click the **登录** button.
3. Register an account on uin88.com (free of charge for PayPal login) and login 
4. After the SSO login, you can choose your desired template, download & replace your entire site.
5. Based on the template, can follow the instruction on uin88.com to configure your Woocommerce site as much as you prefer.
